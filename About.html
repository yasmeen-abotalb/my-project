A bout The resturant

MR FOOD is a contemporary American eatery with British Brasserie influences. A gathering space, it serves breakfast through late night, so early birds and night owls are all welcome. The culinary and cocktail offerings have an international flare, but there are definite nods to our British roots (cheers!). The space can easily accommodate larger dining parties, or provide a more intimate experience for couples. The Kitchen at Commons Club also has an extensive beverage program including carafe cocktails, beer flights, wines by the glass and more.
